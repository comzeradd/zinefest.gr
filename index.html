Athens Zinefest

Athens Zinefest began in 2013 as a private initiative and now is organized by a group of ziners, that aspire to multiply through the years. The festival is organized once a year without sponsors or ads and includes only non-commercial zines. Our goal is the self-organization and dissemination of the culture of fanzines opposite to trends that periodically afflict the ziners-univerce. It is important for us to create an alternative economy, a playback of diy culture and a solidarity network where creation and freedom are not followed by commercial standards and contend to find a way of expression.

Participation in the group is open. On the occasion of the Athens Zinefest 2016 we created a brochure (only in greek unfortunately) about everything that is open in discussion in the community of ziners. Read and send us a message if you want to get involved.

Until now completed:
